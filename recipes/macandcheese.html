Vegan Macaroni and Cheese Recipe

Truly the most delicious macaroni and cheese I've yet to try! You wouldn't even believe it's 100% VEGAN! Yeah you heard me right alright! This delicious and creamy macaroni and cheese is not only delicious but also completely dairy free so you can indulge without feeling guilty. It's super easy to make and you'll have the whole family asking for more

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups- raw cashews
  • 1/2cup- nutritional yeast
  • 16 oz box- elbow macaroni
  • turmeric
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • salt
  • lemon juice
  • water
  • vegan butter
  • panko breadcrumbs

Instructions

  1. Pour boiling hot water over cashews and allow to soak for up to 30 minutes.
  2. While cashews are soaking start bringing a pot of water to boil and add in your pasta cook approximately 7-8 minutes you want the pasta to be aldente.
  3. When cashews are done soaking drain and rinse them and add them to a blender or food processor along with the nutritional yeast,tumeric, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, lemon juice, and water. Blend until you have reached a creamy consistency.
  4. Once pasta is cooked through drain and rinse pasta and add to a casserole or baking tray along with our creamy cashew sauce and mix well.
  5. In a small bowl whisk together the melted butter and panko breadcrumbs. I like to sprinkle in a little shredded vegan parm. This is optional.
  6. Put your mac and cheese in a preheated oven at 350 degrees and cook for approximately 15 minutes/ Do not over bake as this will dry out your mac and cheese.
  7. Lastly serve hot and enjoy!
