Lentil Stew Recipe

Super easy and hearty lentil stew. This recipe is made from just a few ingredients but is packed with tons of nutrients and flavors! This is a perfect meal for when you're feeling a little lazy but are still in the mood for something comforting. More likely than not you already have all these ingredients in your pantry! Let's take a look and I hope you enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Lentils
  • White Onions
  • Celery
  • Garlic
  • Crused Tomatoes
  • Sweet Potatoes
  • Vegetable Broth

  1. Heat up oil in a skillet and add in onions, celery and garlic and sautee until soft and fragrant.
  2. Add in the remainder of the ingredients and stir thoroughly.
  3. Add spices to taste and adjust accordingly.
  4. Bring the mixture to a boil then bring down to a simmer and cover. Let cook for around 30-35 minutes or until lentils and potatoes are cooked through and tender.
  5. Remove one cup of the mixture and add to a blender, blend until smooth and then return to pot and stir.
  6. Serve alone or on a bed of tumeric coconut rice like I did and enjoy!
