Vegan Enchiladas Recipe

Feeling a little spicy today! So we're going to go ahead and make these delicious vegan enchiladas. These enchiladas are not only delicious but super packed with nutrition. Made with only staple pantry ingredients these enchiladas are super budget friendly and enough to feed the whole family.Let's go ahead and get cookin!

Ingredients

  • Black Beans
  • Corn
  • Sweet Potatoes
  • Red Onion
  • Jalapeno
  • Garlic
  • Spices (paprika,chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, onion powder, salt and pepper
  • Tortillas
  • Green enchilada sauce
  • Shredded Vegan Cheese

Instructions

  1. Heat some grapeseed oil in a pan over medium high heat.
  2. Add in the onions and cook until translucent.
  3. Add in the jalapenos, garlic and diced sweet potatoes along with 2 tbsp of water and cover cooking until sweet potatoes are tender.
  4. Once sweet potatoes are tender add in your black beans and corn and cook through for a couple of minutes.
  5. Add in the spices and stir cooking through for a few more minutes, turn off heat and set aside.
  6. To assemble enchiladas first you want to warm your tortillas so they are easier to handle.
  7. Fill the tortilla with enchilada filling and roll tightly
  8. Lay your rolled up enchilada fold down into a baking dish and repeat with all the rest of the tortillas.
  9. Top enchiladas with a generous amount of salsa verde and top with shredded cheese.
  10. Bake in a preheated oven at 350 degrees for 20 minutes.
  11. Remove from the oven and top with your favorites I used enchilada slices and homemade cashew sourcream!
  12. Serve and enjoy!
